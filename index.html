The Person Behind the System

Taylor Tabile

10 years building marketing systems. Now I make them run themselves.

Most people selling "AI automation" have never actually run a marketing account. I have — for a decade. I built campaigns from zero, connected tracking systems, wired CRMs to ad platforms, and stayed in the account long enough to learn exactly which parts of the job are repetitive enough to hand off to a machine.

I started in SEO at Big Leap, then moved through Hawke Media, Scrapbook.com, and Pipeline Agency — each role adding a layer: paid ads across Google, Meta, and Microsoft; e-commerce attribution; B2B lead generation; full CRM systems and drip campaigns. Along the way I kept noticing how much of marketing is the same tasks done over and over — and how much of a business owner's week disappears into them.

So now I build the systems that take those tasks off your plate. AI handling the follow-up, the qualifying, the reporting, the routing — wired through clean integrations so your tools finally work as one. Not a pile of disconnected apps. A system.

Today, as Co-Founder of North Vista Marketing, I work directly with business owners who want their marketing to run without them babysitting it — and who want to make decisions from a dashboard, not a gut feeling. Real automation, built around your business, by someone who knows what the work actually looks like.

What I Automate

06 Systems
01
AI Workflows & Agents

Custom AI workflows built on tools like Claude and ChatGPT — drafting replies, qualifying leads, summarizing calls, generating content, and handling the repetitive thinking work. Wired into your stack through real API connections, not copy-paste.

02
Lead Capture & Nurture Automation

Every lead caught, scored, routed, and followed up automatically — the second they come in. Drip sequences and instant responses that move prospects toward a signed contract without anyone remembering to hit send.

03
CRM & Pipeline Automation

End-to-end CRM setup with stages, triggers, and lead scoring that update themselves. Your pipeline stays clean and current automatically — so nothing stalls and nothing falls through the cracks.

04
Data, Dashboards & Decisions

GA4, Search Console, pixels, server-side events, and ad-platform APIs pulled into one live dashboard. Automated reporting that turns scattered numbers into a single, trustworthy view — so every decision is data-informed.

05
Paid Ads & SEO on Autopilot

Google, Meta, and Microsoft campaigns with automated tracking, budget rules, and revenue-based reporting — plus SEO and AI-search (GEO) systems that keep your content working while you sleep.

06
Integrations That Tie It Together

The part most people skip: making your tools actually talk. Custom API connections and automations that link your CRM, ad accounts, forms, and AI — so the whole system runs as one instead of a stack of disconnected apps.
